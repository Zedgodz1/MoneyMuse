HSBC Life Wealth Accelerate

Visual Summary of Key Features

This is a simplified overview. For full details, please refer to the official Product Summary.

Premiums go to IUA during ICP, then to AUA. Bonuses are credited as shown.

25-Year Minimum Investment Period (MIP)

Initial Contribution Period (ICP): Years 1-4

Regular Premiums & Start-up Bonuses go to IUA.

Years 1-4 (ICP)

Start-up Bonus (to IUA):

  • Year 1: 25% of Regular Premium
  • Years 2-4: 40%/year of Regular Premium
  • (Total: 145% of 1 annual premium)

Premium Holiday: Not Allowed

Partial Withdrawal (from AUA, if funded by top-ups): 7% PWC

Years 5-14

Regular Premiums now go to AUA.

Premium Holiday: Allowed (up to 84 months in MIP)

Partial Withdrawal (from AUA): Up to 2 Free (then 7% PWC). *Impacts Power-up Bonus eligibility.*

Premium Reduction: Allowed (BRC applies). *Impacts Power-up Bonus.*

Years 15-25 (End of MIP)

Power-up Bonus (to AUA, monthly):

  • 1.00% - 1.05% p.a. of Total Account Value
  • (Rate depends on premium tier)

Partial Withdrawal (from AUA): Additional free withdrawals available (total 5 by Year 25). Then 7% PWC. *Impacts Power-up Bonus.*

Year 26 Onwards (Post-MIP)

Loyalty Bonus (to AUA, monthly):

  • 0.90% p.a. of Total Account Value

Partial Withdrawal (IUA or AUA): No PWC. *Impacts Loyalty Bonus.*

Premium Holiday: Unlimited

Key Ongoing Fees:

  • Account Maintenance Fee (AMF): 3.4% p.a. of IUA value (During MIP only)
  • Investment Management Fee (IMF): 1.0% p.a. of AUA value (Throughout policy)
  • Fund Management Charges: Varies by fund (Implicit)
  • Early Encashment Charge (EEC): On IUA if surrendered during MIP (reduces yearly, 8% in Yr 25)

30-Year Minimum Investment Period (MIP)

Initial Contribution Period (ICP): Years 1-5

Regular Premiums & Start-up Bonuses go to IUA.

Years 1-5 (ICP)

Start-up Bonus (to IUA):

  • Year 1: 30% of Regular Premium
  • Years 2-4: 40%/year of Regular Premium
  • Year 5: 50% of Regular Premium
  • (Total: 200% of 1 annual premium)

Premium Holiday: Not Allowed

Partial Withdrawal (from AUA, if funded by top-ups): 7% PWC

Years 6-14

Regular Premiums now go to AUA.

Premium Holiday: Allowed (up to 84 months in MIP)

Partial Withdrawal (from AUA): Up to 2 Free (then 7% PWC). *Impacts Power-up Bonus eligibility.*

Premium Reduction: Allowed (BRC applies). *Impacts Power-up Bonus.*

Years 15-30 (End of MIP)

Power-up Bonus (to AUA, monthly):

  • 1.25% - 1.30% p.a. of Total Account Value
  • (Rate depends on premium tier)

Partial Withdrawal (from AUA): Additional free withdrawals available (total 7 by Year 30). Then 7% PWC. *Impacts Power-up Bonus.*

Year 31 Onwards (Post-MIP)

Loyalty Bonus (to AUA, monthly):

  • 1.10% p.a. of Total Account Value

Partial Withdrawal (IUA or AUA): No PWC. *Impacts Loyalty Bonus.*

Premium Holiday: Unlimited

Key Ongoing Fees:

  • Account Maintenance Fee (AMF): 3.4% p.a. of IUA value (During MIP only)
  • Investment Management Fee (IMF): 1.0% p.a. of AUA value (Throughout policy)
  • Fund Management Charges: Varies by fund (Implicit)
  • Early Encashment Charge (EEC): On IUA if surrendered during MIP (reduces yearly, 8% in Yr 30)

This visual summary is for illustrative purposes and based on the HSBC Life Wealth Accelerate Product Summary (Ver 2.0). Investment involves risks. The value of units can fall as well as rise. Buying a life insurance policy is a long-term commitment. An early termination usually involves high costs and the surrender value payable may be less than total premiums paid. Please consult a financial advisor and refer to official documents for complete details.
