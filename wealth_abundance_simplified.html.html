HSBC Life Wealth Abundance

Simplified Visual Summary (10-Year MIP)

10-Year Minimum Investment Period (MIP)

Account Basics: Regular Premiums to Regular Premium Account (also receives bonuses). Top-ups/RSPs to Top-up Account.

Policy Year 1

Start-up Bonus: One-time bonus of 8% - 12% of 1st year Regular Premium (based on premium amount), to Regular Premium Account.

Flexibility: Premium Holiday/Reduction not allowed. Partial Withdrawal not allowed.

EEC: 100%. PWC: 100% (if withdrawal were possible).

Policy Years 2 - 3

Year 2: Premium Holiday/Reduction/Partial Withdrawal not allowed.

Year 3 (from 25th policy month):

  • Partial Withdrawal: Allowed from Regular Premium Account (PWC applies, e.g., 80% in Yr 3, unless Free Partial Withdrawal). Min. $250.
  • Free Partial Withdrawal Benefit: 2 free withdrawals (each up to 6% of Regular Premium Account value) available during Yrs 3-10.

Year 3 (from 37th policy month):

  • Premium Reduction: Allowed (BRC applies).
  • Premium Holiday: Allowed (max 60 months in MIP, PHC currently Nil).

EEC: Yr 2: 100%, Yr 3: 80%. PWC: Yr 2: 100%, Yr 3: 80%.

Policy Years 5 - 10 (End of MIP)

Power-up Bonus (Yrs 5-10): 0.1% p.a. of Regular Premium Account value, paid monthly to Regular Premium Account.

Flexibility: Premium Holiday, Partial Withdrawal (PWC applies, e.g. 8% Yrs 6-10, or Free), Premium Reduction (BRC applies) continue to be available under their respective rules.

EEC: Reduces annually (e.g., Yr 5: 50%, Yr 10: 8%).

PWC: Reduces then stabilizes (e.g., Yr 5: 40%, Yrs 6-10: 8%).

Policy Year 11 Onwards (Post-MIP)

Loyalty Bonus: 0.3% p.a. of Regular Premium Account value, paid monthly to Regular Premium Account.

Flexibility:

  • Premium Holiday: Unlimited.
  • Partial/Regular Withdrawal: Allowed from Regular Premium or Top-up Account. No PWC.

EEC: 0%. AMF reduces to 0.60% p.a.

Key Ongoing Fees (Summary)

Account Maintenance Fee (AMF): 2.10% p.a. (during MIP) / 0.60% p.a. (after MIP) on Regular Premium Account value.

Premium Charge (Top-ups/RSP): 3%.

Fund Management Charges: Vary by fund (implicit in unit price).

Note: Power-up/Loyalty bonuses can be suspended for 12 months due to withdrawals, premium holidays, or premium reductions as per policy terms.
