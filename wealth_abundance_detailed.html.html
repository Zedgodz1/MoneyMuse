This plan has a Minimum Investment Period (MIP) of 10 Years.

1. Product Overview

HSBC Life Wealth Abundance is a whole life, regular premium Investment-Linked Plan (ILP) with a Minimum Investment Period (MIP) of 10 years. It provides insurance protection up to Age 99 and allows investment in various ILP Sub-Funds.

The plan is designed for long-term wealth accumulation, combining investment growth potential with insurance coverage. It's available in SGD and USD. Remember, as an ILP, your investment is subject to risks, and early termination can be costly.

Key Objective: To offer a blend of insurance protection and investment opportunities, with bonuses to reward policyholders over a 10-year MIP and beyond.

2. Account Structure

The plan utilizes two accounts for managing your premiums and investments:

Regular Premium Account

  • Regular Premiums are allocated here.
  • Start-up, Power-up, and Loyalty Bonuses are credited here.
  • Subject to Account Maintenance Fee (AMF), Partial Withdrawal Charge (PWC) during MIP, and Early Encashment Charge (EEC) during MIP. Premium Holiday Charge (PHC) may apply if imposed by HSBC Life.

Top-up Account

  • Recurring Single Premiums and Top-up Premiums are allocated here.
  • Subject to a 3% premium charge upon allocation.
  • Withdrawals are generally not subject to PWC or EEC.
  • Not eligible for Start-up, Power-up, or Loyalty Bonuses.

3. Bonuses for Your Journey

HSBC Life Wealth Abundance offers bonuses to enhance your policy's value, credited to the Regular Premium Account.

Start-up Bonus

A one-time bonus based on your Regular Premium paid in the first Policy Year.

Power-up Bonus

Paid monthly from the end of the first Policy Month of Policy Year 5 until the end of the 10-year MIP.

Rate: 0.1% per annum (0.1%/12 monthly) of the prevailing Account Value of the Regular Premium Account.

Suspended for 12 months following: Partial Withdrawal from Regular Premium Account, Premium Holiday, or Regular Premium reduction during MIP.

Loyalty Bonus

Paid monthly starting from the end of the first Policy Month *after* the 10-year MIP expires (i.e., from Year 11 onwards).

Rate: 0.3% per annum (0.3%/12 monthly) of the prevailing Account Value of the Regular Premium Account.

Suspended for 12 months following a Partial or Regular Withdrawal from the Regular Premium Account.

4. Fees and Charges (10-Year MIP)

It's important to understand the fees. Most are deducted by unit cancellation.

Premium Charge

- Regular Premiums: 0% (100% allocated).

- Recurring Single Premiums & Top-up Premiums: 3% of premium paid (deducted after allocation). Guaranteed.

Premium Holiday Charge (PHC)

Currently Nil. HSBC Life reserves the right to impose a PHC (not more than 50% of prevailing Regular Premium) on missed Regular Premiums during MIP with 30 days' notice. This would be deducted from the Regular Premium Account.

Account Maintenance Fee (AMF)

Charged monthly from Regular Premium Account, on its account value. Not guaranteed.

- During MIP (Years 1-10): 2.10% per annum.

- After MIP (Year 11 onwards): 0.60% per annum.

Monthly AMF = (Annual AMF Rate / 12) x Account Value of Regular Premium Account.

Bonus Recovery Charge (BRC)

Charged if Regular Premium is reduced during the 10-year MIP. Deducted from Regular Premium Account. Not guaranteed.

Formula: `[Current Start-up Bonus $ - Reduced Start-up Bonus $] x (1 - (Months Premium Paid / 120 months))`

Insurance Charge

No separate insurance charge. The cost of insurance is factored into the product structure.

Partial Withdrawal Charge (PWC)

Imposed on withdrawals from Regular Premium Account during the 10-year MIP (Policy Years 1-10). Deducted from withdrawal amount. Not guaranteed (rates can be revised).

Not applicable to Top-up Account withdrawals or after MIP. Free Partial Withdrawal Benefit available (see Flexibility).

Early Encashment Charge (EEC)

Imposed on Regular Premium Account if policy is fully surrendered or terminates (other than by death/TI) during the 10-year MIP. EEC rates are guaranteed.

EEC does not apply to Top-up Account.

Other Charges

- **Switching Fee:** Currently Nil. - **Redemption Fee:** Currently Nil. - **ILP Sub-Fund Annual Management Fee & Other Charges:** Charged by fund managers, vary by fund, reflected in unit price. Not guaranteed.

5. Insurance Protection & Key Benefits

Death & Terminal Illness (TI) Benefit

Sum of:
(i) Higher of [101% of total Regular Premiums paid (less cumulative partial/regular withdrawals from Regular Premium Account)] OR [Account Value of Regular Premium Account]
(ii) PLUS Account Value of Top-up Account.

TI max S$3M aggregate. Exclusions apply.

Accidental Death Benefit

If death by Accident before Age 75, pays sum of:
(i) Higher of [200% of total Regular Premiums paid (capped S$2M or equivalent), less cumulative partial/regular withdrawals from Regular Premium Account] OR [Account Value of Regular Premium Account]
(ii) PLUS Account Value of Top-up Account.

Higher of Death Benefit or Accidental Death Benefit is paid. Exclusions apply.

Life Replacement Option (LRO)

Request to replace Life Assured up to 3 times, subject to conditions.

Free Partial Withdrawal Benefit

After first 2 Policy Years and during MIP (i.e., Years 3-10):

  • Entitled to **2 free Partial Withdrawals** from Regular Premium Account without PWC.
  • Each free withdrawal capped at **6%** of the Account Value of Regular Premium Account at time of exercise.
  • Exercising this benefit suspends Power-up/Loyalty Bonus for 12 months.

Maturity Benefit

At Policy Expiry Date (Age 99), pays 100% of Total Account Value, less outstanding charges.

6. Plan Flexibility (10-Year MIP)

Premium Payments

Min. Annual Regular Premium: S$6,000 / US$6,000. Monthly (S$500) for SGD only.

- Regular Premium cannot be increased after commencement.

- Regular Premium cannot be reduced during first 36 policy months (Years 1-3).

- From 37th Policy Month (Year 4 onwards): Can apply to reduce Regular Premium (max 25% of original, BRC applies during MIP, Power-up Bonus suspended for 12 months). Cannot increase later.

Premium Holiday

Temporarily stop Regular Premiums.

  • First 36 Policy Months (Years 1-3): Not allowed. Policy lapses if premium unpaid (EEC applies).
  • From 37th Policy Month to end of MIP (Years 4-10): Allowed, up to Max. Premium Holiday Duration of 60 months.
    • Premium Holiday Charge (PHC) is currently Nil but HSBC Life can impose it.
    • Power-up Bonuses forfeited during holiday (can be reinstated if missed premiums are back-paid).
  • After MIP (Year 11 onwards): Unlimited Premium Holiday allowed, no PHC.
Top-up Premiums & Recurring Single Premiums (RSP)

Allowed from 13th Policy Month, before Life Assured Age 70. Min. $100 (multiples of $10). Max RSP is 25% of prevailing Regular Premium (RSP not for USD). 3% premium charge. Allocated to Top-up Account. Not allowed during Premium Holiday.

Withdrawal of Units

Partial Withdrawal:

  • Allowed from 25th Policy Month (Year 3 onwards).
  • Min. amount $250 (multiples of $10).
  • From Policy Year 6 to end of MIP, each withdrawal from Regular Premium Account capped at 50% of its prevailing value (including past withdrawals).
  • Remaining Regular Premium Account value must be ≥ $3,600 (Minimum Holding Amount).
  • Withdrawals from Regular Premium Account during MIP (Years 3-10) subject to PWC, unless it's a Free Partial Withdrawal (2 allowed, each up to 6% of Regular Premium Account value).
  • No PWC for Top-up Account withdrawals.
  • Impacts Power-up/Loyalty Bonus eligibility for 12 months.

Regular Withdrawal:

  • Allowed after MIP (Year 11 onwards). Min. annual S$1,200/US$1,200 (or S$100 monthly for SGD).
  • Remaining Regular Premium Account value must meet Minimum Holding Amount.
  • Impacts Loyalty Bonus eligibility for 12 months.

Full Surrender: Possible anytime. EEC applies to Regular Premium Account if surrendered during MIP.

Switching & Dividends

- **Switching ILP Sub-Funds:** Allowed. Min. switch $1,000 per fund, min. remaining $1,000. Max 10 funds. No fee currently.

- **Dividend Distribution:** For dividend-paying funds, option to reinvest (default) or cash payout (min S$30 total).

7. Suitability and Key Risks

This Plan May Suit You If:

  • You are looking for a medium to long-term investment (10-year MIP).
  • You want a blend of investment growth potential and life insurance.
  • You are comfortable with investment risks.
  • You can commit to regular premium payments (min. S$6,000/US$6,000 p.a.).

Key Risks to Consider:

  • Investment Risk: Value of units can fall; principal loss is possible.
  • Long-Term Commitment: Early termination (during 10-year MIP) is costly due to EEC/PWC.
  • Fees & Charges: AMF, PWC, and fund charges are not guaranteed and can be revised.
  • Bonus Conditions: Power-up and Loyalty bonuses are conditional.

Protected under Policy Owners’ Protection Scheme in Singapore. Always consult a financial advisor.
