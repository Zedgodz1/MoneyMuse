HSBC Life Wealth Voyage

Simplified Visual Summary

Account Basics: Regular Premiums initially go to the Regular Premium Account (which also receives bonuses). Top-ups go to the Top-up Account.

15-Year MIP

Key Periods & Bonuses

ICP (Yrs 1-2): Regular Premiums to Regular Premium Account.

Start-up Bonus (Yrs 1-2): Up to 20% + 20% (based on ARP) of Regular Premium to Regular Premium Account.

Power-up Bonus (Yrs 6-15): 0.20% p.a. of Regular Premium Account value (monthly).

Loyalty Bonus (Yr 16+): 1.00% p.a. of Regular Premium Account value (monthly).

Key Fees

AMF: 2.30% p.a. (during MIP, special calc. last 3 yrs), then 1.00% p.a. (on higher of initial/current ARP x lower of Policy Yr/MIP).

IMF: (Not explicitly stated for Wealth Voyage's Regular Premium Account, but applies to underlying funds).

EEC: Applies during MIP on Regular Premium Account (e.g., Yr 1: 100%, Yr 5: 47%, Yr 10: 22%, Yr 15: 5%).

PWC: During MIP on Regular Premium Account (e.g., Yr 3: 50%, Yr 9: 5%, Yr 15: 5%).

PHC: Applies during MIP after free period (e.g., Yr 3: 74%).

Top-up Premium Charge: 3%.

Flexibility Highlights

Premium Holiday: Not in Yrs 1-2. From Yr 3 (25th month): 24 months free, then PHC applies during MIP. Unlimited after MIP.

Partial Withdrawal: From Yr 3 (25th month). PWC applies during MIP on Regular Premium Account. No PWC on Top-up Account or after MIP.

Premium Reduction: From Policy Year 5 (BRC applies during MIP).

20-Year MIP

Key Periods & Bonuses

ICP (Yrs 1-2): Regular Premiums to Regular Premium Account.

Start-up Bonus (Yrs 1-2): Up to 20%/40% + 25%/50% (based on ARP) of Regular Premium to Regular Premium Account.

Power-up Bonus (Yrs 3-20): Tiered: 0.10% (Y3-5), 0.30% (Y6-10), 0.50% (Y11-15), 0.70% (Y16-20) p.a. of Regular Premium Account value (monthly).

Loyalty Bonus (Yr 21+): 1.10% p.a. of Regular Premium Account value (monthly).

Key Fees

AMF: 2.15% p.a. (during MIP, special calc. last 4 yrs), then 1.00% p.a. (on higher of initial/current ARP x lower of Policy Yr/MIP).

IMF: (Applies to underlying funds).

EEC: Applies during MIP on Regular Premium Account (e.g., Yr 1: 100%, Yr 5: 56%, Yr 10: 28%, Yr 20: 3%).

PWC: During MIP on Regular Premium Account (e.g., Yr 3: 50%, Yr 10: 10%, Yr 20: 5%).

PHC: Applies during MIP after free period (e.g., Yr 3: 98%).

Top-up Premium Charge: 3%.

Flexibility Highlights

Premium Holiday: Not in Yrs 1-2. From Yr 3 (25th month): 24 months free, then PHC applies during MIP. Unlimited after MIP.

Partial Withdrawal: From Yr 3 (25th month). PWC applies during MIP on Regular Premium Account. No PWC on Top-up Account or after MIP.

Premium Reduction: From Policy Year 8 (BRC applies during MIP).

Loyalty Bonus (Yr 26+): 1.20% p.a. of Regular Premium Account value (monthly).

Key Fees

EEC: Applies during MIP on Regular Premium Account (e.g., Yr 1: 100%, Yr 5: 67%, Yr 10: 39%, Yr 25: 5%).

PWC: During MIP on Regular Premium Account (e.g., Yr 3: 50%, Yr 10: 10%, Yr 25: 5%).

PHC: Applies during MIP after free period (e.g., Yr 3: 85%).

Top-up Premium Charge: 3%.

Flexibility Highlights

Premium Holiday: Not in Yrs 1-2. From Yr 3 (25th month): 24 months free, then PHC applies during MIP. Unlimited after MIP.

Partial Withdrawal: From Yr 3 (25th month). PWC applies during MIP on Regular Premium Account. No PWC on Top-up Account or after MIP.

Premium Reduction: From Policy Year 10 (BRC applies during MIP).
